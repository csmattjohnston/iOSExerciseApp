Idea

Who likes working out alone? I know I do not. I have a passion for working out and think that living a healthy lifestyle is important. According to Gallop, 2015 was a record high for adult obesity rates in the United States reaching a staggering 28%. After reading these numbers, I wanted to create an app that allows users to motivate each other, track each others goals and go to the gym together. People who have a motivated partner at the gym as a companion are more likely to attend the gym. This is why I started to create an iOS workout app.

Implementation

I am fairly new to Swift and iOS programming. I have done a lot of research and watched many videos to start to create my dream mobile app. I am currently in stage 1 of my development. I have an idea, back end, and user stories that I want to create. The source code on Github is just a sample of what I have created so far.
